My Favorite Restaurants in Austin

Austin may not be my favorite city, but it is probably the only city I can name five restaurants in. I am not much of a foodie. This site is all about my top five restaurants in Austin Texas.

Here are they are, in no particular order.

  • Titayas
  • Phoenicia Bakery and Deli
  • Sazan Ramen
  • China Family
  • Checkos
